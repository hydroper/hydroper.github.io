About

Hydroper is driven by only one person, Matheus Dias de Souza. The organization name is a consequence of the title “Hydro Thunder” and the sequence “per frame”. This organization was preceded and followed by other names; the most recent substitute was “Hand on Cloud”.

Hydroper is an entertainment organization initially focused on video production, but now focused on game development.

Hydroper is a result of years of interacting with different software and gameplay. Hydroper started as a YouTube channel in 2012.
